Overview

DerbyFish turns real fish catches into a living digital economy.

Verified Catches (Fish Records)

Every time an angler verifies a fish, a permanent record is created.

  • Species, size, date/time
  • Public & private data fields
  • Owned forever by the angler

Species Coins (FishDex)

Each species has its own coin. 1 coin is added for every verified catch.

  • Walleye â†’ WalleyeCoin
  • Scarcity: rare fish = fewer coins
  • Built-in encyclopedia metadata per species

FishCards

Tradeable cards that reveal randomized private data about real catches.

  • Rarity based on revealed info
  • Cards can be bought, sold, and traded
  • Angler earns royalties

Bait (Fishing Credits)

1:1 USDC-backed stablecoin used for:

  • FishCard packs
  • Shop purchases
  • Swapping between species coins

FishDex Intelligence

Biological

Lifespan, diet, predators, and more.

Geographic

Native waters, migration, invasive risk.

Regulatory

Size limits, seasons, licenses by region.

Recreational

Angler fight rating, best baits, taste, and difficulty.

Conservation

IUCN status, threats, protected regions.

Scientific

Active research, population trends, gaps in data.

Marketplace & Royalties

FishCard trades generate royalties for the angler. Bait used across all purchases.

  • Pack opening & rarity reveals
  • Merch + FishCard shop integrations

Security & Privacy

All records and trades are secured and verified. Sensitive catch data is only revealed by chance.

  • Royalties without identity leaks
  • Commit-reveal privacy protection
